The story behind Dexi

Hi, I'm Victor Jatto Akachukwu, a passionate and I've walked in your shoes. As a freelancer, content creator, and digital marketer, I've experienced firsthand the challenges that creative professionals face in today's fragmented digital landscape. am also a Python Backend Engineer currently pursuing my diploma in Backend Engineering at AltSchool Africa 👨🏾‍🎓 (Class of 2025).

After years of juggling multiple platforms, struggling with trust issues, and watching amazing talent get buried in crowded marketplaces, I knew there had to be a better way. That's why I'm building Dexi. I build backend systems, create content around tech, and contribute to open-source projects. I'm also an expert in cybersecurity and automation, documenting my learning journey, tech tools, and motivational AI projects on my socials.

As someone who's experienced the challenges of building professional connections in the tech space, I understand the need for a platform where creative professionals and developers can showcase their skills, build their brand, and connect with meaningful opportunities. That's the vision behind Dexi.

Our Mission

To empower every creative professional with the tools, visibility, and connections they need to thrive in the digital economy.
